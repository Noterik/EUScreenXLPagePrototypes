MYEUSCREEN

MyEUscreen is an ongoing development in the EUscreenXL project, which aims to provide tools for engaging with the EUscreen content for anyone interested. In order to get your personal account and to start using MyEUscreen, please sign up below. MyEUscreen provides currently mainly an option to make your own audiovisual collections and the possibility to store your viewing history. In the later phases of the EUscreenXL project, more MyEUscreen features will be provided - by being a member you can get updates of the latest features as soon as they are released. We hope you enjoy MyEUscreen!
